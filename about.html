Who is Rares?

I, Rares, am a 19 year-old Junior at MSU studying information science.
I am originally from Romania, but I've lived in the United States for over 15 years.
My main hobbies and favorite pass-times are:

  • Singing
  • Traveling
  • Fragrances
  • Cooking

I have traveled from Europe to the US from a very young age, and I travel
as often as I can, ideally once a year if not more. I have worked as a perfume
salesman before at a mall, and ever since I've fallen in love with fragrances,
and I'm always researching different and new fragrances. I've learned how to
cook these past few years, ever since I've moved to MSU, and it's one of the
most relaxing and rewarding hobbies I've discovered, and I can't wait to learn
even more recipes! As far as singing goes, I think that it's one of my most favorite
hobbies and passions I have. I've sung at church, and by myself at home but recently
I've started taking professional voice lessons to better learn how to do different
voice techniques, and how to better control my voice. All of these hobbies have
become a part of my identity, and make me happier than ever. Once again, welcome
to my blog, It's been a pleasure to introduce myself and I hope you enjoy reading
thorugh my posts and content!
